Short Story of the game
You are going on a date with a man. But this man is not quite your taste. 
You want to reject him so bad but he is too stubborn to understand. 
So you have to tell him in a distasteful way.


Instruction
Point and Click

You need to click to select the combination to piss your date.
And you can deselect by clicking what you do not want.
When you can finally choose the combination, click the Enter button to see his reaction.
